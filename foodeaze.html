FoodEaze

The app that caters to you

Description

FoodEaze is the single app that allows user to order delivery, find food trucks in real time, request catering bids, share trusted reviews and discover the most craved food festivals across the country. Users no longer have to download and scroll through dozens of apps to find the best bang for your buck.
